Learn about this attraction

Its of no-doubts that the most religious land of all in the ancient life includes the largest, most esteemed religious site ever constructed till date, Karnak Temple! where you’d feel the spiritualism of a holy city, in 3 main temples & some smaller ones sanctifying God Amun, his wife Mut, & son Khonsu. Wandering around 10 pylons, you’d magically watch engraved stories & rituals, some still retains their original colors. Starting with the last, incomplete pylon - the current main entrance - with rows of sphinxes that lead you to the great court - Kiosk of Taharqa - where only one great column standing still... to statues of Ramses II, temples of Ptah, Osiris & Mut & the Barque chapel of Ramses III, where eight Osiride statues of the king are lined, ending up with Khonsu temple, mainly built of 28 columns & a row of sphinxes! You’d definitely skip beats once you step the Hypostyle hall; the world’s largest room of any religious building, built of 143 gigantic columns! Along with another one of a kind recorded-wonder “The Sacred Lake”; the home for sacred geese & the idea of creation provenance. Finally! The experience you should-NOT miss is Egypt’s second-built Sound & Light show of this great holy city’s historical milestones.
